Shinpei Ajiro

Hello, I am Shinpei Ajioro. I am eighteen-year-old boy who is the main character of the series "SummerTime Rendering". I am good at cooking and attends a cooking school. I am a childhood friend of Ushio Kofune and Mio Kofune. I am a kind and caring person who is willing to help others.

Email: shinpei@summertime.com

Past History

He returned from Tokyo to his hometown, Hitogashima, for the first time in two years to attend the funeral of his childhood friend Ushio Kofune. In the past, Shinpei lost his parents in an accident as a child and was received by the Kofune family.

The anime plot

After two years away, Shinpei returns to Hitogashima to attend the funeral of his childhood friend Ushio. On the boat, he dreams of her asking him to find her and protect Mio.
